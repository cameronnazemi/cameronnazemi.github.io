Tappy Health: iOS App


Tappy Health Website

Tappy is a haptic (vibration) therapeutic iOS application I developed for Kids and Teens suffering with mental (and sometimes physical) disorders.


Download it here on the App Store

Tappy uses vibrations mixed with visual experiences to help users quickly achieve relaxation. It has received many reviews raving about its effectiveness in combating Anxiety, Stress, Panic Attacks, etc. Tappy is currently in the Top 200 Family Apps on the App Store! You can also read some of the reviews on the App Store page to see how it has impacted Kids and Teens lives.

During this project, I designed and built Tappy using Xcode, SwiftUI, Core Haptics, and Affinity Designer.

Project Description

Tappy is a fidgeting app designed to help you reduce stress and anxiety in a fun and satisfying way! As you tap, not only will you enjoy an incredible haptic response, you'll notice that your count increases as well! This will be important for unlocking features. More features will be added in the future so if you already surpass the tap count it'll be automatically unlocked for you! There is also so much more to Tappy! Check out the rest of the app to discover ways to be mindful, improve mental health, reduce stress, and remove anxiety. You'll find great fidget games and tools (like a Fidget Spinner!) that give satisfying haptic feedback responses! Tappy aims to be your one stop fidget app for relaxing, focusing, and just having fun!
